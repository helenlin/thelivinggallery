THE LIVING GALLERY was founded in April of 2012 by Nyssa Frank. We are dedicated to offering emerging artists and creators a platform for their dreams to manifest. We strive to create an environment based on collaboration, not competition. The Living Gallery is a space where passion meets action. We hope to always be alive with new artwork, events and ideas.
